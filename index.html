Game of life

Rules

  1. Any live cell with two or three live neighbours survives.
  2. Any dead cell with three live neighbours becomes a live cell.
  3. All other live cells die in the next generation. Similarly, all other dead cells stay dead.

Static figures

Figures that remain unchanged

Oscillators

Figures whose state repeats after a certain number of generations greater than 1

Spaceships

Figures whose state is repeated, but with some offset

Guns

Figure with repeating states, additionally creating moving shapes

Puffer trains

Moving figures with repeating states that other shapes leave behind as traces
